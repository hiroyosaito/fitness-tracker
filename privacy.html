Privacy Policy

GetFitDaily  ·  Effective date: April 7, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 7, 2026

This policy explains what personal data GetFitDaily collects, why, how it is protected, and what rights you have — including the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time.

1. Who Is Responsible for Your Data

GetFitDaily is operated by:

  • Name: Hiroyo Saito
  • Email: saito.hiroyo@gmail.com

Hiroyo Saito is the data controller as defined under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact the email above.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data you directly provide when using the app:

  • Account data: email address and password (or your Google account identifier if you sign in with Google)
  • Workout data: exercise names, weights, repetitions, sets, duration, distance, activity type, effort level, companion names (first names only, if entered), class names, notes, and workout dates

We do not collect your real name, phone number, physical address, payment information, precise location, or any health data beyond what you choose to log.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the GetFitDaily service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store, display, and analyse your workout history
  • To generate the reports and progress charts you view in the app
  • To send password reset emails when you request them

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app. We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

GetFitDaily uses the following third-party processors. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on our behalf.

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure, hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the United States. Supabase complies with GDPR as a data processor. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Vercel (web hosting) — the app is served via Vercel's global network. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google (optional sign-in) — if you choose to sign in with Google, Google shares a unique identifier and your email address with us. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Data stored in Supabase (US) is transferred outside the European Economic Area. This transfer is covered by Supabase's Standard Contractual Clauses with AWS.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Passwords stored as bcrypt hashes — we never store or see your plain-text password
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database level — each user can only access their own data, even if they call the API directly
  • Session tokens stored in your browser's local storage and sent only over encrypted connections

7. Data Retention

Your account and all associated workout data are kept for as long as you have an active account. If you request deletion of your account, all your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can correct inaccurate data directly in the app, or contact us for account-level corrections
  • Right to erasure — you can delete individual entries in the app at any time. To delete your entire account and all data, email saito.hiroyo@gmail.com
  • Right to data portability — you can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format (CSV) by emailing us
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest by contacting us
  • Right to restriction — you can request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email saito.hiroyo@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

GetFitDaily does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We use your browser's local storage solely to keep you signed in between sessions. This data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties. You can clear it at any time by signing out or clearing your browser's site data.

10. Children's Privacy

GetFitDaily is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
